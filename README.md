- **Backend Framework**: Flask (Python)
- **AI/ML**: OpenAI GPT-4 API
- **Database**: Firebase Firestore
- **OCR Engine**: Tesseract with PDF2Image
- **Deployment**: Ngrok tunneling for development, Heroku-ready for production

### Key Features

#### 1. Intelligent Document Processing
Upload invoices, contracts, bills, or any document. The system extracts:
- Payment deadlines
- Contract expiration dates
- Action items and obligations
- Financial risk assessments

#### 2. Proactive Life Management
Instead of reacting to missed deadlines, Ai-Life-admin:
- Predicts what needs attention before it becomes urgent
- Suggests optimal times to complete tasks
- Identifies patterns in your administrative workload

#### 3. Enterprise-Grade Features
- **Multi-user collaboration**: Share access to documents and tasks
- **Compliance monitoring**: Track regulatory requirements and certifications
- **Risk assessment**: AI-powered analysis of potential administrative pitfalls
- **Audit trails**: Complete history of all document analyses and actions

### Use Cases

- **Personal Finance Management**: Track bills, subscriptions, and payment deadlines
- **Business Administration**: Manage contracts, licenses, and compliance requirements
- **Legal Document Tracking**: Monitor case deadlines and filing requirements
- **Healthcare Management**: Track insurance renewals, prescription refills, and medical appointments
- **Property Management**: Monitor lease agreements, maintenance schedules, and tenant communications

### Why Ai-Life-admin?

In today's fast-paced world, administrative tasks pile up quickly. Missing a single deadline can result in late fees, lost opportunities, or compliance issues. Ai-Life-admin acts as your intelligent administrative assistant, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks while providing actionable insights to help you stay ahead.
